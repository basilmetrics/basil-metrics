When managing the paid search architecture for this premium retail brand, the primary objective was capturing bottom-of-funnel, high-intent buyers for a specific high-value product line. We needed to ensure we were getting in front of customers exactly when they were actively searching and ready to purchase.

We deployed a laser-focused Google Search campaign that successfully captured 949 highly qualified clicks. By ruthlessly optimizing our keyword targeting and ad copy, we achieved a stellar Click-Through Rate (CTR) of 4.30%, while driving the Cost Per Click (CPC) down to an incredibly efficient 4.75 THB.

What makes this campaign especially notable is the immense potential for scalability. We achieved these highly efficient numbers while our search impression share remained below 10% due to budget constraints—proving that with scaled ad spend, this conversion engine has massive room to grow without losing its efficiency.
