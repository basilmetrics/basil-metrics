Building a loyal community on TikTok through organic content alone can be a slow, unpredictable grind. For this ambitious F&B brand, we decided to engineer immediate, measurable momentum to push them closer to a massive milestone: the 100k follower mark.

We launched a highly targeted follower-acquisition sprint designed to capture the attention of food enthusiasts and convert them into long-term audience members. The velocity of the campaign was immediate. In just 17 hours and 33 minutes, we pushed the account from 94.2k to 94.6k followers—a rapid acquisition of 400 new, targeted fans.

This translates to a blistering pace of nearly 23 new followers per hour, or roughly 547 per day. By proving out this high-velocity acquisition model, we successfully transitioned the brand off of a slow organic drip and placed them on a predictable, data-backed trajectory to cross the 100,000 follower threshold in under two weeks.
