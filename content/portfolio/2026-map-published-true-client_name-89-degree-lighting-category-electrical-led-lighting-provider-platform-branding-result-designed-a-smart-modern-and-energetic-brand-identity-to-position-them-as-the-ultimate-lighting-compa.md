89 Degree Lighting is a comprehensive provider of electrical and LED lighting equipment. They needed a visual identity that communicated their core philosophy: the "Perfect Angle of Light".



The strategic goal was to establish 89 Degree not just as a standard supplier, but as a true "lighting companion" (เพื่อนคู่คิดด้านแสงสว่าง) for a diverse audience that includes homeowners, interior designers, and electricians. The branding needed to highlight their dedication to quality, manufacturing precision, and design that meets both architectural and practical user needs.



We developed a complete brand identity that captures their defined personality: Smart, Modern, Energetic, and Accessible. The design system includes a versatile logo suite featuring primary, secondary, and submark variations. To ensure the brand stands out in the hardware and architectural space, we anchored the visual identity with a bold, high-contrast color palette utilizing a vibrant yellow-green (#D7E44E) and a deep, professional blue (#173881).
