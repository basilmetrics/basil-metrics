## The Challenge

A delivery-first F&B brand came to us with zero social presence and a launch deadline. No followers, no content library, no brand voice — just great food and a tight budget.

## The Strategy

We built a 45-day TikTok sprint combining organic content hooks with paid amplification. Every video was engineered around the first 2 seconds — the make-or-break moment on TikTok.

Key pillars: behind-the-scenes kitchen content, delivery unboxing hooks, and creator-style voiceovers that felt native to the platform.

## The Amplification

Once we identified the top 3 organic performers, we put paid budget behind them immediately. This is the move most brands miss — waiting too long to boost proven content.

## The Result

1M views across the account in 45 days. 3,200 new followers. A 4.2% engagement rate against a platform average of 1.8%.

## What This Means for Your Brand

TikTok rewards consistency and speed. The brands winning are the ones who test fast, identify winners early, and amplify without hesitation.
